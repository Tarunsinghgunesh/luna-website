2What gets deleted

Once your deletion is confirmed, we permanently remove:

  • Firebase Authentication — your login record, including linked Google Sign-In.
  • Firestore — every cycle entry, symptom log, mood note, and profile field.
  • Firebase Storage — any attached files or exported reports stored in your account.
  • Partner Mode connections — any active link to a partner account is severed immediately.
  • Notification tokens — device tokens used for reminders are revoked.

Anonymized, aggregate analytics that cannot be traced back to you (such as overall app crash rates) may be retained for product improvement.
