What LUNA does

Everything your cycle needs. Nothing it doesn't.

Five quiet tools that work together, so understanding your body never feels like a chore.

Cycle Tracking

Log periods, flow, and symptoms in seconds. LUNA learns your pattern over time and predicts your next cycle, fertile window, and ovulation day with growing accuracy.

Wellness

Mood, energy, sleep and symptom trends in one calm timeline — so patterns become visible instead of invisible.

Partner Mode

Invite someone you trust to see a gentle summary — mood, phase, and how they can help — without ever exposing your raw entries.

Smart Notifications

Quiet, well-timed reminders for logging, upcoming periods and predicted ovulation — never noisy, always relevant.

Privacy First

Your health data is encrypted end-to-end and never sold or shared with advertisers. You can export or permanently delete everything, any time, from inside the app.

Inside a cycle
