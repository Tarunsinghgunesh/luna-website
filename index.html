What LUNA does

Everything your cycle needs. Nothing it doesn't.

Five quiet tools that work together, so understanding your body never feels like a chore.

Cycle Tracking

Log periods, flow, and symptoms in seconds. LUNA learns your pattern over time and predicts your next cycle, fertile window, and ovulation day with growing accuracy.

Wellness

Mood, energy, sleep and symptom trends in one calm timeline — so patterns become visible instead of invisible.

Partner Mode

About LUNA

A companion, not a checklist

LUNA was built on a simple idea: understanding your body shouldn't feel clinical, and caring for it shouldn't feel like admin.

Our story

Built by TK Web Solutions, for anyone who has ever felt unheard by a health app

LUNA is developed by TK Web Solutions, an independent web and app development studio based in Bharatpur, Rajasthan, India. We build products that are quietly useful — no dark patterns, no clutter, no data harvesting dressed up as "personalization."

LUNA started as a question: why do most cycle trackers feel like spreadsheets wearing pink icons? The answer became a design principle — calm interfaces, honest predictions, and privacy that isn't a marketing slogan.

Inside a cycle

LUNA follows the four phases with you

Each phase brings different energy, mood and symptoms. LUNA labels them clearly so you always know where you are.

Menstrual

Rest-focused. LUNA tracks flow intensity and cramps so patterns are easy to spot.

Follicular

Energy usually rises. A good window for logging mood and planning ahead.

Ovulation

LUNA predicts your fertile window using your logged history and cycle length.

Luteal

Symptom tracking helps you notice PMS patterns before they arrive.

The short story

Tarun Singh is the founder and solo operator of TK Web Solutions, a web design and app development studio based in Bharatpur, Rajasthan. He handles everything end-to-end — design, development, and support — building products that are meant to be used daily, not just downloaded once.

Why LUNA was created

After watching people in his own life struggle with cycle-tracking apps that felt cluttered, ad-heavy, or careless with sensitive health data, Tarun set out to build something different: an app that respects the person using it. LUNA is the result — a tracker built with the same attention to detail as a product from a much larger team, but with the personal accountability of a single founder standing behind it.

Vision
